## Hard rules

- Copy rule: no em-dashes, no colon-heavy phrasing, short labels, honest and
  lightly human voice.
- Speed rule: no interaction blocks the UI over 100ms.
- Both light and dark themes must be beautiful. Use design tokens only, no raw
  colors, no rgb() or hex in the surface or component styles.
- Keep the scores honest: abstain on thin data, never hardcode a number. Never
  mutate scheduling state. Everything must still work with AI off. Score hues
  stay a data language, never decoration.
