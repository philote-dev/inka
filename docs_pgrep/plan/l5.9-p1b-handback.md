  "Make a flashcard", right panel is "Matching cards".

  20 blueprint topics one card at a time, with a "Card N of 20" counter,
  prev/next steppers, and the topic in focus (amber, the Memory data language).
  A successful add advances to the next topic and clears the editor, so the flow
  keeps leading forward. The just-built matching cards stay on the right.

  matching the mockup and the IA. Confirmed keep with Frank.
- Verified live in light and dark.
